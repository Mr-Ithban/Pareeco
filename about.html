Company Overview

Who Is PAREECO?

PAREECO Trading & Contracting L.L.M is an innovative enterprise operating in Qatar with a strong presence in the construction and electro-mechanical sectors. The company has built a reputation for delivering high-quality projects through professionalism, reliability, and technical expertise.

  • 18+ years of industry experience in Qatar and the GCC region
  • Full-spectrum MEP, construction, and maintenance solutions
  • Trusted by clients across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors
  • International quality and safety standards on every project
  • Skilled and certified engineering workforce
  • Commitment to innovation, efficiency, and client satisfaction
